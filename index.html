About Us

At Brick at Branch, we are dedicated to creating beautiful and functional outdoor spaces that enhance your home and garden. Based in Worcester, we specialize in hard landscaping to make your garden easy to navigate, enjoyable, and affordable. Whether you have a specific design in mind or want us to transform your garden from scratch, we’ll bring your vision to life.

But we don't stop at landscaping—we go beyond to create ecosystems that encourage local wildlife and birdlife. Our planting schemes are carefully tailored to Worcester’s unique climate and soil conditions, ensuring your garden flourishes year-round. Plus, we offer ongoing care and maintenance to keep your garden vibrant for years to come.

With a degree in economics from Aberystwyth University, I initially pursued a career in finance in London. However, my love for nature and the outdoors led me to a new path—one focused on the beauty of designed landscapes in urban and rural spaces. In the UK, our gardens cover an estimated 433,000 hectares—more than four times the size of our national nature reserves. At Brick at Branch, we take pride in unlocking the full ecological potential of your garden, creating spaces you’ll love to spend time in.

Working alongside Alan Barrat of A J Garden Solutions, we have over 4 years of experience in designing and building features like arbours, fences, raised beds, patios, and water features. We’ve completed projects ranging from small city gardens (100m²) to larger properties (1000m²). You can view examples of our work in the review portfolio section.

Garden Maintenance

In addition to landscaping, we offer comprehensive garden maintenance services to keep your outdoor space in top shape. Our maintenance packages include regular lawn mowing, hedge trimming, scarifying, weeding, seasonal planting, and more. We’re happy to tailor our services to meet your specific needs, and we work hard to ensure everything is completed to the highest standard.

  • Fortnightly lawn mowing and hedge trimming
  • Scarifying, weeding, and seasonal planting
  • Tailored maintenance plans to suit your garden

I provide maintenance services at £25 per hour, with competitive rates for waste removal based on your location.
